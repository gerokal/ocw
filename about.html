About


Gabor Estefan is a certificated coach and teacher who helps millennials to become creative professionals and finally get money for what they love doing.

After having enough from the soul sucking jobs and arrogant managers he decided to make his way out of the rut and take control of his life and create his own dream life style and career. Since the wake up call his mission is to become someone who can easily break through limitations and by using his talent and creativity he wants to help others to experience the freedom of choice in their life.

He studied day and night to find out how to get where he wanted to be, he collected all the knowledge and organised into a programme that he wants to share with you.

Gabor doesn’t believe in quick fixes, shortcuts and easy success. It is not just about you need to be happy and then your life will be beautiful. He knows that if you want to achieve something earlier, you need a crystal-clear vision, focus, determination and you need to work very hard.

He knows it is not easy so instead of traditional coaching he provides unique and fun experiences that can make the process more enjoyable.

His solutions to your problems are fresh, simple and easy to apply them into your everyday life so you can save time and energy and focus on what is really important for you.

He knows everyone is different and we all need different strategies to get the results we want.
